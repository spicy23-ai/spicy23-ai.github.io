🗑️Book Deletion

  • Authors may delete their own uploaded books at any time from the "My Books" section.

  • Authors are strongly advised to request a payout for any available earnings before deleting a book.
  • Deleting a book does not affect copies already purchased by readers before deletion — those readers will still be able to access their purchased copy in "My Purchases."
